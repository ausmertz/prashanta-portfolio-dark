About Prashanta

A visual storyteller
with a cinematic soul

Hi, I'm Prashanta Maharjan — a freelance photographer and videographer based in Kathmandu, Nepal. With over 5 years of experience working with FotoMagic, event venues across Kathmandu, and independent clients, I bring a cinematic and emotional storytelling approach to every project.

From the quiet intimacy of a bridal preparation to the charged energy of a live concert — I specialise in capturing real feeling in every frame. My work spans weddings, events, fashion, and branded content.
